# Code of Conduct

## Introduction
This Code of Conduct outlines the community standards and professional expectations for all contributors to the ForensicAI project. We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all.

## Expected Behavior

- **Respectful Communication:** Treat all individuals with respect, kindness, and consideration. Engage in constructive discussions and avoid personal attacks.
- **Collaboration:** Foster a cooperative environment. Offer help where needed, and be open to receiving suggestions and feedback.
- **Inclusivity:** Embrace diversity. We encourage participation from individuals of all backgrounds and experiences, and we actively support an inclusive community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment:** Harassment of any kind is not tolerated. This includes offensive comments, personal attacks, and any form of discrimination.
- **Disruption:** Disruptive behavior in meetings, discussions, or online forums is unacceptable. This includes shouting, trolling, or any form of intimidation.
- **Impersonation:** Do not impersonate or misrepresent others. All participants are expected to represent themselves honestly.

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness any unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers immediately. Sensitive matters will be treated confidentially, and all reports will be taken seriously.

## Consequences
Unacceptable behavior may lead to consequences including, but not limited to, temporary or permanent suspension from the community. We will take necessary actions to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all members.

## Acknowledgment
By participating in this project, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. Let’s work together to create a positive and professional community for digital forensics contributors!  

---  
This Code of Conduct is inspired by open-source community standards and contributions from the wider digital forensics community.
